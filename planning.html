Accommodations are limited on Cape Cod during the peak summer season, so please make your reservation as soon as possible.

If you choose one of these options, please reference the Murphy-Murphy wedding when you call for reservations.

Recommended Accommodations

Ocean Edge Resort (Wedding reception location)
2907 Main Street
Brewster, MA 02631
508-896-9000

A limited amount of the following rooms are available at the wedding rate:

  • Hotel Guest Rooms (ocean side of the resort) $400 per night
  • Arbor One Bedroom Villa (golf course side of resort) $325 per night
  • Britterige One Bedroom Villa (golf course side of resort) $350 per night

There is a two night minimum for these accommodations. There is a resort shuttle bus that will transport you around the resort.

Skaket Beach Motel
203 Cranberry Highway
Orleans, MA 02653
508-255-1020

Recently renovated guest rooms with rates starting at $150 per night, including continental breakfast.

There is a two night minimum for these accommodations. The motel is 2 miles from the wedding site at Ocean Edge and we will provide bus transportation for guests staying at this motel.

Seashore Park Inn
24 Canal Road
Orleans, MA 02653
508-255-2500

Guests staying two nights will receive the AAA, Senior Citizen discounted rate. Guests staying one night will pay the regular rate. Rates begin at $150 per night, including continental breakfast. One night minimum for these accommodations. The inn is 5 miles from the wedding site at Ocean Edge and we will provide bus transportation for guests staying at this inn.
