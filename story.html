When they first arrived at Holy Cross their freshmen year there were a few things Katie and Joe both planned on achieving by graduation: getting smarter, having fun, experiencing different things, and making new friends. Neither of them planned on meeting the person with whom they would spend the rest of their life. But as fate would have it, they did, and they couldn't be happier!

Too much credit should not be given to the fact that they were placed in the same dorm, switched into the same philosophy class, or that freshman girls love to gossip about crushes--- but many factors contribute to their life together.

Katie first saw Joe at a holiday dinner in Mulledy dorm, where he, as dorm cochairman, was serving abnormally large carrots and beef. That is where she first noticed his charming personality... or maybe it was because he had just prepared a large feast, and she would be able to eat it. Either way, Joe is the only guy capable of putting up with her awkwardness, and who shares the same sense of humor as her. Philosophy 101 brought Katie and Joe together, she has the notes written during class about Joe to prove it.

For Joe, the story is a little different. See, it's hard to not notice the girl with bright blue eyes, extremely long hair, and wardrobe that seemed to consist of only spandex and torn sweatshirts. Such a girl was clearly unattainable for Joe, but fortune favors the bold. Not only did Joe get the answer he was looking for when he asked Katie out that summer after freshman year, but 5 years later he got the answer to the question he had on his mind from the beginning, "Yes!"
